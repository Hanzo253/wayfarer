About Wayfarer....

Wayfarer was founded and created by four well polished amazing Java Immersive Scholars by the names of Sasha Carr, David Doan, Johnny Agugua and Kevin Brown on December of 2021. Wayfarer was created to give the audience an opportunity to take a moment of their day and view our radiant gorgeous world from the eye lens of the creator. This site is a place for exploring and seeing others' experiences while also being able to interact with those who have decided to post their experience. Wayfarer follows Love and Peace so always lead with that model when interacting with fellow account holders, we pride ourself on maintaining a Peace And Love environment......so stay a while and enjoy our glamorous site.
